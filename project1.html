12-Speed Gearbox Redesign

Mechanical Design & Manufacturing

TL;DR

  • Objective: Reduce weight of an existing gearbox by 15% without sacrificing torque.
  • Tools Used: SolidWorks, ANSYS (FEA), Mastercam.
  • Result: Achieved a 18% weight reduction; prototype successfully manufactured and tested.

1. The Problem

The previous iteration of the gearbox was over-engineered, leading to unnecessary weight which reduced the overall efficiency of the drivetrain. The challenge was to identify areas where material could be removed safely.

2. Engineering Process & Iterations

Initially, I attempted to hollow out the primary shafts. However, preliminary Finite Element Analysis (FEA) in ANSYS showed this would lead to catastrophic failure under peak load. I pivoted my approach to optimizing the housing geometry and changing the casing material to a lighter aluminum alloy.
